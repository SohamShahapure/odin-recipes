Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons margarine
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 cube chicken bouillon, crumbled.
  • 1 1/2 cups boiling water.
  • 1 cup 2 % milk
  • ground white pepper to taste.

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Melt butter and margerine in saucepan over medium-low heat.
  3. Add flour and crumbled bouillon; stir untill well-blend , 1 to 2 mins.
  4. Continue to cook and stir until thickened and lightly browned, about 5 minutes.
  5. Increase the heat to medium and whisk in water until smooth.
  6. Stir in milk; cook and stir until hot and thickened, 2 to 3 more minutes.
  7. Season with white pepper.
